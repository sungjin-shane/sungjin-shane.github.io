Leadership

What is your experience of compassionate (or not) leadership?

When I worked as a team manager I had a compassionate leadership. This is not a my opinion. It is perception of many other colleagues in the company. However I think that sometimes, company do not want excessive compassionate leadership because it causes negative disorder in work. I believe that the compassionate leadership is essential for every leader because leader is mother or father of team. Thus leaders should understand their memebers' voice, and feel for them. But, in my experience, some members needed strong leadership because all member did not follow me by compassionate leadereship. A few member might try to take advantage of my characteristic. In this case, When I showed 2 or 3 times compassionate leadership to them, most of them tried to change their negative mindset to coorperate mind, but few member did not try to change their attitude. So, I had to show strong leadership appropriately.

Do the multiplying goodness meditation. How did you find it?

It is difficult to understand others' mind. When we meet new people, we normally have wariness against them. Sometimes, people do not trust them. I think it is the same situation in work environment. For example, team member do not want to trust new boss. On the other hand, the boss do not want to trust abilities of the members. However, in organization like department, the trust between boss and members is the most significnat factor. In this case, the multiplying goodness meditation is very useful solution to resove this complex and negative situation in department. In my case, when I meet a new person or join in new department, having a positive mind for new people or environment cause positive effect to understand each other. Imagine, when we meet together in bootcamp which is the best mindset? Before attending the bootcamp, we need to prcatice the multiplying goodness meditaion. If we do this a lot, eventually, we will trust every member because we understand each other and care together.
