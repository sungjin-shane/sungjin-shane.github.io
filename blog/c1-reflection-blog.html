Deep impression of EDA

Software development is a team sports such as soccer,basketball and rugby. It is not a individual games like tennis
When we build a project team there are people who have great experience in IT, while otheres do not have sufficient IT knowledge.
This is a very similar with playing team sports becuase every member can not have superior ability.
Also, individuals have different IT skills and knowledge in the project, as if soccer team member has different role during playing game.

Some people said that this is a my program and I will make them by myself. However, this is not a suitalble mindset in EDA.
In IT project or IT organization, all tasks are developed by coorperation between colleagues, even experienced project manager learn
from junior developere.
We can make successful projects and output from working together. we can exchange their advantages from accepting others opinion
I discovered that humble mindset is very significant because it can cause growing interatction among member and it can creative thinking.
