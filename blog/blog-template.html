The Story about Shereef's Fireside Chat

Nowadays, IT industry is a fast-growing market in Auckland. Thus, there is a variety of IT institutes as well as many e-learning systems. I think that most these education systems are operated by Restaurant(Customer) mindset which was mentioned by Shereeef. This is because students buy a curriculum such as learning javascript, CSS and HTML, and also individuals learn these from the teacher. Sometimes, they pay online courses and learn from lecture who exist on the screen, as if customer order food in the restaurant. In this case, there is no creative interaction between them. Everything just depends on teacher's ability and only individuals' capability.

However, after watching the Shereef's video, I felt a strong impression and difference. I believe that DBC/EDA is totally different learning system to others. It is a much more creative system.

> "Make together" is totally different mindset as if people make food in the kitchen with their friends together. I was thought from Sheereef's Chat that I did not order Bootcamp package. I am a member of Bootcamp, and we will develop it by working together.
