Whatever happened to softly & tenderly?

14th April 2018

Eventually, you will want to get to the point where everything is for Jesus. Easy? Hardly. If you say everything is for him. then you better be prepared to go through what he went through Exaustion, Rejection, Rersecution, Betrayal, Death. Yes death-not of your body, but of your old life. You are going to be asked to leave it behind and get a new one. Are you ready for that? You can do it in his strenght.

I also agree that it is really hard to leave old things. When I faced with difficult problems every day I am still use my old things to solve the issues but I have to try to leave my way, and apply a new way that he is everything. Not your interseting, and physical greed in lives. Recently, I am getting better than before, and I begin to meet a new world through getting throw it away.

Yes. As a matter of fact, it is hard-and it is going to get harder. But it is kind of like a hard rain. Changes comes next.

We will not always get applause

16th April 2018

There are a lot of people who ask "Why God why?" over and over. God almost always has the same answer. "Because I said so". There may not be banners with your name streched across the door when you get back. Also, there is no reason that you should go. However, we go because god said "Go". We do it because God said "Do it". That is the reward.
"We do it because God said"

God will not always send you someplace pretty, You will not always get applause. You are a servant of God. Now, pray to be sent anyway. Are you willing to spend and be spent; not seeking to be ministered unto, but to minister?

You are able to find fast way and choose easy way. You may say that this not my way, and I do not want to choose it.
We will not always make a successful story. Sometines we need to feel sense of pain in the bitter fail, and we need to be under other people's feet as a doormat.
