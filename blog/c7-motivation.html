How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

Most people pursue pleasure, and I also spend most of time on seeking just enjoyable things. However, it does not sustainalbe happyness because once the enjoyable stimulus ceases, then our happiness will return our default set point in mind. For example, when we go to hunting, and we take a big deer we can gain super pleasure. But after that, we will easily lose our pleasure, and people should seek another stimulus to maintain their pleasure.
I realized from reading the chaper 6 that setting up higher target is the best way to make more sustainable pleasure.
This is because our tasks in works are able to be stimulus that can make sustainable hapyness. Although, it is difficult to realize the importace of setting a higher goal once we are working toward fulfilling our higher purpose, the progress itself is the reward like bonus.
There is an example in company. When people work in the company they have their own target for a year or month known as KPI(Key Performace Indicator). I know that in a way, it causes stressful situation to employees, otherwise, most employees are able to gain a satisfaction from working toward fulfilling their target.
