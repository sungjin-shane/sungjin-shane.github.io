-Create tiny behaviors

There was a amazing video that impressed me [Link]
Dr. BJ Fogg's said in the video "When we know how to create tiny habits, we can change your life foever."
As we can see the above image, 3 elements at same moments affect to people's behaviors which lead to outcome

What was interseting, most people could experience failure due to focusing on high motivation and it is hard to continuously maintain.
Dr, BJ Fogg recommend people to use low motivation effect because it is easier to succeed and it makes much more excellent behaviors.
Making the behaviors tiny with effective triggers is the key point.

What did you find interesting?

After reading and watching various ideas for time management, many people said that the pomodoro is a template.
It means that we can modify it work for individuals. When I applied the pomodoro timebox to my study or work
I felt that sometimes, the 25 miniutes interval was too short or sometimes, it is too long. It was getting be a distractions
Thus, I thought I need modification about timebox duration...
Firlstly, I classified my work as working category such as reading websites, watchaing a videos and coding programmes, etc.
Secondly, I simulated suitable timming scheme from my last history of study and work. (It just like a heuristic method.)
Lastly I was able to define each interval for my classified work type. My thought trying to apply the same timming scheme was wrong.
