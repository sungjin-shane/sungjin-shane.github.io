Create tiny behaviors

There was a amazing video that impressed me [Link]
Dr. BJ Fogg's said in the video "When we know how to create tiny habits, we can change your life foever."
As we can see the above image, 3 elements at same moments affect to people's behaviors which lead to outcome
Behavior = Motivation * Attitude * Trigger (This is significant)
What was interseting, most people could experience failure due to focusing on high motivation and it is hard to continuously maintain.
Dr, BJ Fogg recommend people to use low motivation effect because it is easier to succeed and it makes much more excellent behaviors.
Making the behaviors tiny with effective triggers is the key point.
This is the example of tiny behaviors that change your life forever. So I decided to make tiny behaviors.
