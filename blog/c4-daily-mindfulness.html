Extend the benefit of midfulness

"A mindfulness meditator needs to do is extend the benefits of mindfulness beyond sitting into every part of life."

What did you learn from your'mindfulness conversation'?How did other person find it?

There are three key points in mindfulness converstion.
1)listening 2) looping 3)dipping
In my case, I often have been argued with my wife. This is because I do not ready for listening.
Also, I got distracted by my own feelings and internal chatter.
I tried to apply mindfullness conversation on conversationing with my wife. It showed amazing results.

It has very simple three components. Firstly, just listening to others' opinion and do not reaction to what other person said. Secondly, we are easily able to check what others said from looping process. It reduce the gap of understanding between you and other person. Lastly, checking in with ourselves is difficult because we have a lot of feelings and internal chatter during the conversation but we can maintain it from acknowledging them. "Know that they are there, try not to judge them, and let them go if they are willing to go."
